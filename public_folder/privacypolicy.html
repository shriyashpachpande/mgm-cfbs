Privacy Policy

At Campus Facility Booking System, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Student ID, name, and email address (if used during login).
  • Booking Information: Details like selected facility, date, time, and booking status.
  • Usage Data: Basic technical info like device type, browser, and access time.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow users to book facilities and manage their bookings.
  • To verify and authenticate users.
  • To provide booking confirmations and notifications.
  • To monitor system usage and performance.

3. Data Sharing

Your personal and booking data is only accessible to authorized campus administrators. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

4. Data Security

All data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access. Only designated admin users have access to sensitive information.

5. Cookies

This platform may use cookies to maintain login sessions and enhance user experience. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

Students have the right to request, update, or delete their booking records. For any such request, please contact the campus IT or administration.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact the campus administration or IT department.
